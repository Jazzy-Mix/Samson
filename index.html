About Me

Agualomunu Samson Seun is a detail-oriented music producer with a deep love for music and sound. My expertise lies in mixing, mastering, and producing dynamic beats, as well as enhancing podcast audio quality. I am committed to elevating audio projects to their fullest potential, ensuring they resonate with audiences and meet industry standards.

Services

  • Mix & Mastering: Enhances clarity, depth, and balance of tracks. Specializes in Afrobeats, Hip Hop, R&B, Pop, and more. Ensures music is polished and ready for release on all platforms.
  • Audio Cleaning for Podcasts: Removes background noise, clicks, pops, and distractions. Improves vocal clarity and overall listening experience. Tailors solutions to fit each podcast's unique style.
  • Beats Production: Creates original, high-energy beats for various genres. Collaborates with artists to produce custom beats aligned with their vision.

Skills

  • Proficient in DAWs: FL Studio, Live, Pro Tools, Logic Pro, Cubase.
  • Expertise in plugins for EQ, compression, reverb, delay and more.
  • Skilled in audio editing, waveform analysis and spectral enhancement.
  • Strong understanding of acoustics and studio setup.

Portfolio Highlights

  • Track Mixing & Mastering: Worked on independent artist releases, ensuring streaming platform standards and emotional impact.
  • Podcast Audio Enhancement: Improved audio quality for popular podcast series, boosting listener engagement.
  • Beat Production: Created instrumental tracks for live performances and studio recordings, helping artists establish their brand identity.
